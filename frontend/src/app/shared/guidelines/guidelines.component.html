Frequently Asked Question

Q1. Are the schemes open for all students enrolled in Indian Universities/ Colleges/ Institutes?

Ans: Yes. The schemes are open for all the students who are current enrolled in an Indian University/ College/ Institute

Q2. Is there any annual income ceiling for students to be included under the scheme?

Ans: Yes, only those students whose annual family income is less than or equal to Rs.4 lakh per annum are covered under the schemes.

Q3. Do we need to upload all the documents while filling the online application form?

Ans: Yes, it is necessary to upload all the documents during application to carry out the verification from our end. Your documents will not be shared with any third party users

Q4. Can students with drop years apply for the schemes?

Ans: Yes. But the gap between two academic years must be less than or eqaul to 3 years.
